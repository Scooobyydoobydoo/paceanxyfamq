WE AMPLIFY ARTISTS AND GIVE VALUE TO COLLECTORS.

Pacemaker is a Membership Club for fine digital art and culture.

WE REWARD COLLECTORS BY GIVING THEM FREE ACCESS TO ALL FUTURE ARTWORK NFT DROPS.

WE AMPLIFY ARTISTS BY PROVIDING ACCESS TO OUR GLOBAL NETWORK & ECOSYSTEM, ENABLING THEM TO BUILD AND SHOWCASE THEIR WORK IN UNIQUE WAYS.

By amplifying our artists and providing long-term value to our collectors, we build a first of its kind membership street art project.

WITH GLOBAL ARTISTS FROM OUR NETWORK AS WELL AS UPCOMING TALENTS FROM OUR COMMUNITY, OUR PACE BOARD CURATES EACH UPCOMING STREET ARTWORK SET FOR THE PACEMAKER HOLDERS.

EACH ARTWORK SET CONSISTS OF SEVERAL NFT EDITIONS AND MIGHT EVEN HAVE 1/1S. PACEMAKER HOLDERS GET FREE ACCESS TO ALL FUTURE DROPS TO CLAIM ONE ARTWORK NFT PER TOKEN.

EACH ARTWORK NFT IS CONNECTED TO A CUSTOM PHYSICAL PRODUCT THAT SHOWS OFF THE ARTWORK. THESE PHYSICAL GRAILS CAN ONLY BE FORGED BY THE SPECIFIC ARTWORK HOLDER. FROM HIGH-QUALITY STREETWEAR AND ACCESSORIES TO SKATEBOARDS AND HYPE GADGETS, EVERYTHING IS POSSIBLE.

Wether you are experienced in crypto or a newbie, we want to welcome you to a secure space that you actually understand.

The ERC-721A token is on the Ethereum Blockchain and file hosting on IPFS.

To honor our earliest and most dedicated supporters, we airdrop three NFTs (one for each phase) prior to mint which grant holders certain perks. Perks differ from phase to phase, with Phase 1 being the rarest, and may include free or discounted mints, access to exclusive physical products and more. Check our Discord for a chance to enter one of the phases or buy them on secondary.

Pacemaker is a street wear brand founded by Till Jagla in 2016. PacemakerNFT is a collection of Pacemaker Rabbits, uniquely generated from over 850 highly-detailed 3D traits! Owning a PacemakerNFT allows you to collect digital street art NFTs through regular drops that can be forged into exclusive physical products.

Each holder is given complete ownership and commercial usage rights of their NFT. You can check out our terms and conditions for further information once the NFT is released.
